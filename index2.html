Yuva Vikas Sanstha

The youth group form initially as a college group from 1997. Group started performing social activities like street play, drama competition etc. Later on group member’s decided to register group as a social organization. At present 50 youth are working enthusiastically in the group as a committed volunteers.

Our Goals

Youth

◆ To conduct different projects for value education and personality development of the youth.
◆ To guide and orient young groups about career opportunities and organize, develop employment providing programmes
◆ Provide young community members with services like play facility , library, study classes for their educational and intellectual development
◆ Enhance different projects for developing active citizens and social workers in youth groups through educating them on issues like social justice, gender equality, secularism and democracy, environmentalism, honesty and unity etc.
◆ To create platform for giving scope for young members’ skills and knowledge.

Children

◆ To conduct different projects for social, physical, psychological and educational development of marginalized sections like children in streets, orphan children, slums and tribal children
◆ Organize and develop programmes for socializing value education
◆ To assess and create awareness about effective government schemes, policies and information regarding children.

Women

◆ To establish legal aid and guidance centre on physical, psychological violence against women
◆ Set up and socialize different programmes for developing leadership skills amongst women
◆ Organize and develop projects for women’s financial development and self sufficiency to promote the as a successful entrepreneurs.

Civil Issues

◆ To set up and conduct different projects on rural and urban social issues like, housing, roads, electricity, health, education etc.
◆ To establish compliant resolution centre for working on social basic and needful services.
◆ To conduct different projects for rural development. To encourage farmers for using governmental schemes and programmes.
◆ To assess and develop compilation of information regarding developmental issues, different sectors at governmental and semi- governmental authorities. Also share resources at different organizations and network like minded organizations for developing projects.
◆ To organize activities concern with ‘Economic and Social Development of downtrodden, tribal, Senior citizen ect.
