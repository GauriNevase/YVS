Yuva Vikas Sanstha

The youth group form initially as a college group from 1997. Group started performing social activities like street play, drama competition etc. Later on group member’s decided to register group as a social organization. At present 50 youth are working enthusiastically in the group as a committed volunteers.

Know More

◆ To conduct different projects for value education and personality development of the youth.
◆ To guide and orient young groups about career opportunities and organize, develop employment providing programmes
◆ Provide young community members with services like play facility , library, study classes for their educational and intellectual development
◆ Enhance different projects for developing active citizens and social workers in youth groups through educating them on issues like social justice, gender equality, secularism and democracy, environmentalism, honesty and unity etc.
◆ To create platform for giving scope for young members’ skills and knowledge.
