Yuva Vikas Sanstha

The youth group form initially as a college group from 1997. Group started performing social activities like street play, drama competition etc. Later on group member’s decided to register group as a social organization. At present 50 youth are working enthusiastically in the group as a committed volunteers.

Our Goals

Youth

We develop youth through value education, career guidance, and employment programs. Our services include education, play facilities, and promoting active citizenship. We provide platforms for showcasing youth skills.

Children

We conduct projects for the development of marginalized children, addressing social, physical, psychological, and educational needs. We organizes value education programs and raises awareness about government schemes for children.

Women

We establish a legal aid center for women facing violence. Our programs nurture women's leadership skills. We initiate projects fostering financial independence, empowering them as successful entrepreneurs.

Civil Issue

We address housing, roads, and healthcare issues. Our resolution center handles social needs. We empower rural areas and compile data to foster development, focusing on marginalized groups' economic and social upliftment.

Yuvak Vachnalay

In Bhuigoan since 1973, due to some uncertain reason, the government library was not in effect. We understood this thing, in August 2005, our co-workers had taken personal loan and started the library again. In library, we had kep t new and important literature for village people. In library till this date 276 members have enrolled their name. Out of which 113 children have enrolled their name for membership of library and 67 economically poor children have enrolled with nominal fees Rs.10
